# Quality Metrics

> Track the quality of your outputs over time.

## Scoring System

/review provides scores across 6 dimensions:
1. **Clarity**: Is it clear and understandable?
2. **Impact**: Does it achieve its goal?
3. **Voice**: Does it sound like you?
4. **Structure**: Is it well-organized?
5. **Accuracy**: Is it factually correct?
6. **Completeness**: Does it cover everything needed?
