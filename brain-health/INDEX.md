# Brain Health

> Track your brain's growth over time.

## Files

- `growth-log.md` - Timeline of brain updates
- `pattern-confidence.md` - Pattern strength tracking
- `quality-metrics.md` - Review score trends

---

## Quick Health Check

Run `/grow` to see your brain's current health metrics.

---

## What Gets Tracked

1. **Knowledge Growth**: New patterns, ideas, experiences added
2. **Pattern Confidence**: How strong your extracted patterns are
3. **Quality Scores**: How well your outputs are rated by /review
4. **Time Savings**: Estimated time saved through pattern application
