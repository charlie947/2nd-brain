# Values & Beliefs

> The core context that shapes everything you create.

## Files in This Folder

- `core-values.md` - Your fundamental values
- `beliefs.md` - What you believe to be true
- `frameworks.md` - Mental models and frameworks you use
- `principles.md` - Guiding principles for your work
- `povs.md` - Point-of-view statements (opinions)

---

## Why This Matters

Your values and beliefs are the foundation of your brand voice. They:
- Shape how you approach topics
- Determine what you emphasize
- Influence your recommendations
- Make your content authentically you

---

## How to Fill This Out

Be specific and opinionated. Generic values = generic content.

**Bad**: "I believe in quality"
**Good**: "I believe speed beats perfection. Ship fast, iterate based on feedback."

**Bad**: "I value honesty"
**Good**: "I tell clients the hard truth even when it costs me the deal. They remember."
