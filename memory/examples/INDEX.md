# Examples Index

- `linkedin/` - Top LinkedIn content
- `instagram/` - Successful Instagram posts/reels

## How to Use

1. Add your best performing content to the relevant folder
2. Include context (what it was for, why it worked)
3. The AI will learn your patterns from these examples

---

## What Makes Good Examples

- Content that got results (engagement, conversions, praise)
- Pieces you're proud of
- Work that represents your best voice
- Content you'd want to replicate
