# Knowledge Repository

> Your idea vault. Never lose a thought again.

## Folders

- `ideas/` - Raw ideas and thoughts
- `concepts/` - Developed ideas and frameworks
- `insights/` - Key learnings and observations
- `papers/` - Research notes and summaries

---

## How to Add Ideas

Use `/add-idea` to capture new ideas, or manually add files:

```markdown
# [Idea Title]

**Date**: YYYY-MM-DD
**Status**: raw | developing | developed | used
**Application**: content | book | speech | client | product

## The Idea

[Your idea]

## Why It Matters

[Why this is interesting or useful]

## Potential Applications

- [Application 1]
- [Application 2]
```

---

## Idea Statuses

- **raw**: Just captured, not developed
- **developing**: Being fleshed out
- **developed**: Ready to use
- **used**: Already applied (note where)
