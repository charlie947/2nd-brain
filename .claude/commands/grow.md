### What Compounds

2. **Quality** - Review scores improve as patterns are applied
3. **Speed** - Familiar patterns execute faster
4. **Consistency** - Brand voice strengthens over time
5. **Insights** - Customer knowledge deepens

### Measuring ROI

- 50%+ time reduction on similar tasks
- Higher first-draft quality (fewer review cycles)
- Consistent voice and positioning

- Complete 3+ full cycles without skipping /learn

### Not seeing time savings?

- Ensure you're applying patterns (check in /plan)
- Review pattern confidence levels
- Complete more cycles to build compound effect

### Quality not improving?

- Review /review feedback consistently
- Update patterns based on what works
